Tableau Project: Profitability Analysis

Overview

This project analyses sales and profitability trends across African regions using Tableau to uncover key insights and patterns.

Interactive Dashboard

Key Insights

  • The Northern Region achieved the highest profit ratio (2.74%), indicating strong efficiency.
  • The Western Region generated the highest sales but had the lowest profit ratio (0.33%), signaling operational inefficiencies.
  • Residential and Commercial segments are the strongest performers, with Residential slightly leading in profitability.

Strategic Recommendations

  • Investigate cost structures in the Western Region to improve profitability.
  • Focus on strengthening the Residential and Commercial segments by tailoring campaigns and customer retention strategies.
  • Expand opportunities in the Northern Region through targeted investments in marketing and infrastructure.
  • Explore partnerships and tailored offerings for the Public segment to unlock additional revenue streams.
